Our Story

A Celebration of
African Heritage

Born from a deep reverence for the vast culinary landscapes of Africa, Afrish Petals was conceived as more than a restaurant — it is a living tribute to the continent's extraordinary diversity of flavours, traditions, and stories.

Our head chef, Amara Osei, trained across Lagos, Marrakech, Addis Ababa, and Cape Town before bringing this tapestry of techniques to Kigali, weaving them into tasting menus that honour their roots while pushing boldly into new territory.

Sourced Locally

Every ingredient chosen from Kenya's finest farms and markets.

Crafted with Intent

Each dish tells a story drawn from African culinary heritage.

Served with Grace

Unhurried, attentive service worthy of every occasion.
